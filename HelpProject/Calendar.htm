The calendar panel allows you to record the menstruation data.

How to use:

  • Click on a date - you can do a mark and the color will change to yellow.
  • Left-Click on a marked date - you can cancel the marking.
  • Double-Click on a date - a pop-up window will show to type the duration of your menstruation this time, and these data will be recorded. The color will changed to pink.
