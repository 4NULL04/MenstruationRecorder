Menstruation Recorder (MR) is a easy-to-use recorder for menstruation periods. It was designed for personal using, and the data stored in local storage was private. Any peeking without permission was illegal.

You can use MR to record your menstruation time with simple clicking, and this software will record the beginning time and duration for you. The strength of MR is the function of graph painting, which will pating the variation curve of menstruation automatically. You can use the result to check your menstruation variation and ameliorate your daily life. MR also provided the functions of taking notes.
